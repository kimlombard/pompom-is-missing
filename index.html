Lilac-Point and Blue-Point Siamese are very similar.

Many people get confused between the Lilac Point and the darker Blue Point, because both are blue-grey.

The difference is that Lilac Points are silvery-grey, whereas Blue Points are a darker, slate-grey, as you can see in the photo below.

Mia, on the left is the Blue Point, and Gizmo, on the right, is the Lilac Point.
